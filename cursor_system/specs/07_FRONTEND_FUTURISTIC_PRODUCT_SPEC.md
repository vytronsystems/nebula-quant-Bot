# Futuristic Frontend Product Spec

## Product modes
- Operator Mode
- Executive Mode

## Branding
- Name: NEBULA-QUANT
- Theme: dual theme
- Language: selectable

## Visual direction
Institutional futuristic quant cockpit.

### Palette
- deep space graphite / midnight blue background
- electric cyan primary
- nebula violet secondary
- controlled laser green for positive states
- technical amber for warnings
- plasma red for critical risk

## Mandatory screens
- Executive Dashboard
- Operator Cockpit
- Venues Overview
- Instruments Control
- Strategies Registry
- Research Lab
- Backtest Center
- Walk-Forward Review
- Paper Trading Monitor
- Live Trading Control
- Orders & Positions
- Risk Center
- Market Regime Monitor
- Audit & Evidence Center
- Incidents & Alerts
- Performance & Analytics
- Settings & System Configuration

## Instrument control requirements
- Add Binance pairs dynamically.
- Activate/deactivate each pair.
- Add TradeStation underlyings dynamically.
- Activate/deactivate each underlying.
- Show paper/live eligibility and current operational state.

## Responsiveness
Desktop first, but fully usable on tablet/mobile.
