Reconciliation Engine Audit

Purpose: Detect divergence between exchange state and internal state (orders, positions, balances).

Implementation

  • nq_reconciliation: OrderReconciliationModule, PositionReconciliationModule, PnLReconciliationModule.
  • runner.run_reconciliation(venue, internal_orders, venue_orders, internal_positions, venue_positions, internal_pnl, venue_pnl, on_alert).
  • on_alert(reason, payload) invoked on order_divergence, position_divergence, pnl_divergence.

Result: PASS — reconciliation detects and reports divergence.
